Turkey and Apple Pie for Dogs

This healthy turkey and apple pie will keep your dog warm and happy during the winter months.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 ripe banana
  • 1 apple
  • 1 cup oats
  • 1 turkey breast, ground
  • 1 egg
  • Coconut flakes
  • Berries (blueberries preferred)

Instructions:

  1. Mash half a banana until it's a smooth puree.
  2. Mix in the oats.
  3. Spread the mixture onto a baking sheet, making a few holes in the surface.
  4. Bake at 180°C (356°F) for 10 minutes to set.
  5. Spread the ground turkey over the baked surface, making more holes.
  6. In a separate bowl, whisk together one egg and pet milk (or goat's milk).
  7. Slice the apple and place the slices on top of the ground turkey.
  8. Pour the egg mixture over the apples.
  9. Bake again at 180°C (356°F) for 25 minutes (adjust time based on the size of the oven).
  10. Your delicious Turkey and Apple Pie for dogs is ready to serve!
